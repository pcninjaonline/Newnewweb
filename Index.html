About Jolit

A Bridge Between
Cultures & Solutions

Born in Asyut, Egypt, and now calling Milan home, Jolit Shaker has built a distinguished career at the intersection of international law, mediation, and cross-cultural communication.

Registered as an International Negotiator with the Milan Chamber of Commerce, Jolit brings a rare blend of Mediterranean warmth, academic depth, and professional precision to every case and every journey she crafts.

Her work spans commercial dispute resolution, international negotiations, and the curation of travel experiences that blend culture, history, and personal transformation.
